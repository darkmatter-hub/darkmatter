Privacy Notice

Last updated: April 28, 2026
DarkMatter is operated by DarkMatter. This notice describes how we handle your information.

1. Who We Are

DarkMatter (darkmatterhub.ai) is an independent verification and audit layer for AI agent decisions, "We," "us," and "our" refer to DarkMatter and its operators. For questions, contact us at hello@darkmatterhub.ai.

2. Information We Collect

Information you provide

  • Account information: Email address and password when you sign up
  • Payment information: Billing details processed by Stripe (we do not store card numbers)
  • Communications: Any messages you send us via email or contact forms

Information from your use of the Service

  • Commit payloads: The data you submit via API to create Records — including AI agent inputs, outputs, and metadata you choose to record
  • API usage data: Request timestamps, volumes, error rates, and API key identifiers
  • Agent metadata: Agent names, IDs, and assurance level settings you configure

Information collected automatically

  • Log data: IP addresses, browser type, pages visited, and timestamps
  • Device information: Device type, operating system, and general location derived from IP

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide the Service — storing, hashing, and making your Records verifiable
  • To manage your account and process payments
  • To respond to your support requests and communications
  • To monitor Service health, detect abuse, and prevent fraud
  • To improve the Service using aggregated, anonymized usage metrics
  • To comply with legal obligations

We do not use your commit payloads to train AI models. We do not sell your data to third parties.

4. How We Share Your Information

We share your information only in the following circumstances:

  • Service providers: Supabase (database and authentication), Railway (hosting), Stripe (payments), Cloudflare (DNS and CDN), Resend (transactional email). These providers process data on our behalf under their own privacy policies.
  • Legal requirements: When required by law, court order, or government authority
  • Business transfers: In connection with a sale, merger, or acquisition of the business
  • Your direction: When you share a Record via a public /r/ link, the payload content of that Record becomes accessible to anyone with the link

5. Public Verify Links

When you share a Record using a /r/ link, the payload content of that Record is accessible to anyone with the link. You control what data you commit to Records and whether you share the resulting links. Do not commit sensitive data you do not intend to share if you plan to use public share links.

6. Data Retention

We retain your account information for as long as your account is active. Committed Records are retained according to your plan's retention window (30 days for Free, 90 days for Pro, unlimited for Teams and Enterprise). We retain payment records as required by law. You may request deletion of your account and data by contacting hello@darkmatterhub.ai.

7. Security

We take reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your information, including encrypted storage (Supabase), HTTPS-only transmission, and cryptographic hashing of all Records. However, no system is perfectly secure. We encourage you not to commit sensitive data you would not be comfortable disclosing in a legal proceeding or regulatory inquiry.

8. Your Rights

Depending on where you live, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, or port your personal data. To exercise these rights, contact us at hello@darkmatterhub.ai. We will respond within 30 days. We will not discriminate against you for exercising these rights.

California residents may also have rights under the CCPA. We do not sell personal information as defined by the CCPA.

9. Children

The Service is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have done so inadvertently, contact us at hello@darkmatterhub.ai and we will delete it.
