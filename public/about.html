About

The independent record for AI systems.

DarkMatter is an infrastructure layer that gives AI agents an immutable, independently verifiable audit trail. Every action is sealed at the moment it happens using a cryptographic hash chain anchored outside your system. The record cannot be altered after the fact by anyone, including DarkMatter.

We built DarkMatter because AI agents are now making consequential decisions, and the records of those decisions live inside the same infrastructure that made them. When something goes wrong, the record is controlled by the party being investigated. That is the problem we solve.

How it works
L1

Tamper-evident hash chain

Every commit is SHA-256 hashed and linked to its parent. Modifying any record breaks every downstream hash, detectable by anyone offline.

L2

Independent timestamp anchor

Checkpoint bundles are anchored via OpenTimestamps. The timestamp proof lives outside DarkMatter infrastructure and is independently verifiable.

L3

Non-repudiation

Commits are signed with a key only you hold before they reach our servers. DarkMatter cannot forge a record on your behalf.
