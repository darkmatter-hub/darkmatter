What we’re building
The problem

AI agents make consequential decisions. No one can prove what they decided.

Audit logs stored inside your own infrastructure have the same credibility problem as auditing your own books. When a dispute starts, the record you relied on is the first thing challenged.

The approach

Independent. Cryptographic. Verifiable without trusting us.

Every commit is hashed client-side, chained to its parent, and stored outside your infrastructure. With L3, it’s signed with a key only you hold — DarkMatter cannot forge it. The offline verifier is open-source.

The model

Infrastructure, not a platform. Complementary to every tool you already use.

DarkMatter works alongside LangSmith, MLflow, Datadog, and your own logs. It’s the layer underneath that makes those records independently trustworthy when it matters.

What we believe
01
The record has to exist before the dispute. You cannot retroactively prove what an AI decided. The audit trail that matters is the one you built before anyone asked for it.
02
Trust should be mathematical, not contractual. A vendor promising your logs are safe is not the same as a system where tampering is cryptographically detectable. We build the second kind.
03
Independence cannot be self-certified. A record stored inside the system being audited cannot be independently verified. DarkMatter’s value comes from living outside your stack.
04
Honesty about what we are. We’re an early-stage product. L3 is live. Billing and SOC 2 are not. We say so on the pricing and security pages rather than hiding it.
05
The verifier should work without us. If DarkMatter went away tomorrow, every proof bundle you exported would still verify offline. The open-source verifier depends on your public key — not our servers.
What’s live today
Core record layer

Commit, chain, verify, replay, fork, export

SHA-256 hash chain, parent linkage, trace IDs, event types, Merkle inclusion proofs. The full chain is replayable and exportable as a portable proof bundle.

L3 non-repudiation

Customer Ed25519 keys. DarkMatter cannot forge your records.

Generate a keypair with darkmatter keys generate. Register the public key. From that point, every commit is signed before it reaches us.

SDK wrappers

One import. Auto-instrumented.

Drop-in wrappers for the Anthropic and OpenAI Python SDKs. Every messages.create() and chat.completions.create() is automatically committed.
