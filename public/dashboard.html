Connect Claude
Your team will use Claude for all AI conversations. Anthropic bills you directly for usage. DarkMatter charges separately for your workspace.
1
Go to console.anthropic.com and sign in (or create a free account).
2
Click API Keys in the left menu, then click Create Key.
3
Give it a name like DarkMatter workspace, copy the key, and paste it below.
4
That's it. Your team can start using Claude immediately.
Not sure where to find it? Open Anthropic console
Your key is encrypted and stored securely. DarkMatter uses it only to send your team's requests to Anthropic. It is never shared with anyone.
Connect ChatGPT
Your team will be able to use GPT-4o alongside Claude. OpenAI bills you directly for usage.
1
Go to platform.openai.com and sign in (or create a free account).
2
On the home screen, click Create API key (or go to platform.openai.com/api-keys).
3
Click Create new secret key, name it DarkMatter workspace, copy it, and paste it below.
4
That's it. ChatGPT will appear as an option for your team in the chat.
Not sure where to find it? Open OpenAI platform
Your key is encrypted and stored securely. DarkMatter uses it only to send your team's requests to OpenAI. It is never shared with anyone.
Invite people to your workspace
Each person will get an email with a link to join. They sign in and can start chatting right away. No setup needed on their end.
Press Enter or comma after each email to add more people at once.
Only people you invite can join. Each person gets their own login. You can remove anyone at any time.
