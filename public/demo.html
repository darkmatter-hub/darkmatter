What you’re looking at
Step 01
Agent commits a decision
At the moment of action, dm.commit() seals the payload outside the agent’s own system. The record exists independently from this point forward.
Step 02
Hash chain locks the sequence
Each commit hashes into the next. Modifying any record breaks every downstream hash — detectable by anyone with the chain and the verifier.
Step 03
Ed25519 signature proves origin
With L3, the customer’s own key signs the envelope before it reaches DarkMatter. DarkMatter cannot forge this signature — only your key can.
Step 04
Verify offline — no DarkMatter dependency
Download the proof bundle and run the verifier locally. Verification requires only the public key — not DarkMatter, not the internet, not your account.
